### Chinese Font Selection
- Sans-serif: fontFamily "Noto Sans TC" (Traditional) or "Noto Sans SC" (Simplified)
- Serif: fontFamily "Noto Serif TC" (Traditional) or "Noto Serif SC" (Simplified)
- Use sans-serif (Noto Sans TC) for modern, clean designs
- Use serif (Noto Serif TC) for traditional, elegant, literary designs
- Mix Chinese serif headlines with sans-serif body text for contrast

### Font Sizing for Chinese
- CJK characters are visually denser than Latin — reduce fontSize by 10-15% compared to English
- Headline: fontSize 48-72 (vs 56-96 for English)
- Body: fontSize 16-20
- Minimum readable: fontSize 14 for Chinese characters

### Line Height for Chinese
- Chinese text needs more lineHeight than English
- Headlines: lineHeight 1.3-1.4
- Body text: lineHeight 1.6-1.8
- Dense paragraphs: lineHeight 1.8-2.0

### Mixed Language Layout
- When mixing Chinese and English, use separate text elements for each
- English subtitle below Chinese headline (or vice versa)
- Keep consistent alignment between both language elements
- English text at 70-80% of the Chinese text fontSize for visual balance

### Chinese CTA Phrases
- 立即報名 (Register Now)
- 了解更多 (Learn More)
- 免費試用 (Free Trial)
- 限時優惠 (Limited Offer)
- 立即購買 (Buy Now)
- 加入我們 (Join Us)

### Full-Width Punctuation
- Use full-width punctuation for Chinese text: ，。！？（）
- Do not mix half-width and full-width punctuation in the same text element
